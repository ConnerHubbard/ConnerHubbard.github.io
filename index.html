Big five personality test 

The big five personality test tests how you measure up in the five personality traits. These are openness (someone’s tendency to think in abstract and complex ways), conscientiousness (someone’s ability to exercise self-discipline), extraversion (the inclination to seek stimulation from the outside word), agreeableness (a person’s tendency to put others needs in front of their own) and neuroticism (a person’s tendency to experience negative emotions).
